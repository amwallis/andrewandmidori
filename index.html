Us

Our first date took place on a cold and snowy January night at an outdoor skating rink in Ann Arbor. Midori quickly grabbed Andrew's hand after stepping onto the ice, and we haven't let go since.

That first hygge winter of exploring bookstores and cooking together led to springtime runs, summer hikes, and autumn cider mill dates, all interspersed with trips to Tampa and Detroit to visit family. After working and studying in separate cities (Seattle, Philadelphia, Ann Arbor, and Atlanta) for over a year, we’re especially happy to be in the same city at last. We’ve enjoyed celebrating many milestones while dating, Andrew’s Master's graduation from Michigan, Midori passing her PhD quals at Georgia Tech, and our Confirmation in the Catholic Church together, but we’re especially happy to be engaged and can’t wait to celebrate our marriage with you soon!


Destination

St. Petersburg, Florida

Known as the "Sunshine City" with 361 days of clear skies per year, St. Petersburg is home to beautiful, award-winning beaches, the world-renowned Salvador Dali Museum, top-rated restaurants and beautiful Fort De Soto Park. It's where we got engaged and we’re excited to spend a weekend with you in a city that holds a special place in our hearts.
