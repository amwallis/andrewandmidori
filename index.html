Lodging

Hilton St. Petersburg Bayfront

We’ve reserved rooms at the Hilton in Downtown St. Pete. It's walking distance from the ceremony (2 blocks) and only a half mile to the restaurants and shops along Beach Dr. downtown.

Airbnb

There are plenty of options avilable if you'd like to explore the St. Pete area from a local's perspective by renting a cozy beach bungalow, house, or apartment downtown. This is a great option for large groups travelling together.
